This folder contains all the code necessary to reproduce the results illustrated in the plots in the paper.

twostate_mrp_example.py and toyMRPexp.py are responsible for producing the results of EmpIRE on the two-state MRP described.

twostate_mdp_example.py and toyMDPexp.py are responsible for producing the results of EmpIRE on the two-state MDP described.

highdim_mrp_example.py and highdimMRPexp.py are responsible for producing the results on the MRP of varying dimensions.
